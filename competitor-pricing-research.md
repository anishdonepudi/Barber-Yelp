## Key Fee Details

### Booksy
- Base: $29.99/mo + $20/staff. A 5-chair shop = ~$110/mo before processing.
- Boost (optional): 30% of first visit from new marketplace clients.
- Card reader: 2.49%+$0.10; Tap to Pay: 2.49%+$0.20; Mobile: 2.69%+$0.30.

### Fresha
- WAS free, shifted to paid subscriptions in 2025. Users angry about the change.
- Solo: $19.95/mo. Team: $9.95-$14.95 per bookable staff/mo.
- Marketplace: 20% of first visit from new clients (minimum $6).
- Processing: 2.19%+$0.20 -- must use Fresha payments (no external processors).

### StyleSeat
- $35/mo flat. Solo-practitioner focused.
- The **$2.35 client booking fee** is hugely controversial -- clients see and pay it.
- Client Connection (marketplace): 30% of service revenue from acquired clients.
- Smart Pricing: auto-raises prices 20% during demand; StyleSeat takes 5.25-5.5% of that.

### Squire
- Tiered: Independent $30-$100/mo; Shop $100-$250/mo. Features locked by tier.
- Processing via Stripe (~2.6%). Hardware pricing not publicly listed.
- 20% marketplace commission on first booking from new clients.
- Clients report being charged a booking fee for online payments.
- 0.5% fee on payroll transactions.

### theCut
- Free plan available (basic booking + payments). Pro = $25/mo.
- 2.75% processing (no flat per-transaction fee). Lowest barrier to entry.
- Growth feature: $10 per new client acquired (optional).
- Owners always free. Built exclusively for barbers.

### Vagaro
- $30/mo + $10/staff. Add-ons (text marketing, forms, website) = $10-$20/mo each.
- Processing: 2.2% (in-person) to 3.5% (keyed-in). Wide range.
- No marketplace commission. 30-day free trial.

### GlossGenius
- Standard: $24/mo (2 users). Gold: $48/mo (9 users). Platinum: $148/mo (unlimited).
- Flat 2.6% processing on ALL transaction types. No per-transaction flat fee.
- Instant payouts available for +1.8% fee.
- Hardware: Tap & Go reader ~$50, Pro Reader ~$299.

### Boulevard
- Enterprise-grade: $158/$263/$369-$421/mo per location. Plus add-ons ($45-$55/mo).
- Processing: 2.65%+$0.15. Annual contracts typical.
- Targets upscale salons, not barbers. Priced out of reach for most shops.

### Square Appointments
- Free tier: 1 location, unlimited calendars. Best truly-free option.
- Plus: $29/mo (multi-location). Premium: $69/mo (resource scheduling).
- Processing: 2.6%+$0.15 in-person. Free magstripe reader; chip reader $59; terminal $299.
- No marketplace, no commission, no client fees.

## What Barbers Complain About Most

1. **"Double-dipping" fees** -- paying monthly subscription AND high processing fees AND marketplace commissions (Squire, Booksy).
2. **Client booking fees** -- StyleSeat's $2.35 charge makes barbers look greedy to their own clients.
3. **Marketplace commissions eating margins** -- 20-30% of a $40 haircut ($8-$12) gone on the first visit.
4. **Feature lockout** -- basic features like waitlists, text reminders, or multi-location gated behind expensive tiers.
5. **Price hikes without notice** -- Fresha's switch from free to paid; Square's processing fee increase.
6. **Poor customer support** -- universal across Squire, Booksy, and Fresha reviews.
7. **Forced payment processing** -- Fresha won't let you use Stripe/Square; you must use theirs.

## Average Barber Income Context

- **Median barber income**: ~$52,000/yr ($4,300/mo)
- **Range**: $35,500 (25th pctl) to $75,000 (90th pctl)
- **Gross revenue** (before chair rent/supplies): $10,000-$20,000/mo for busy barbers
- A barber doing $8,000/mo in card transactions at 2.6% pays ~$208/mo in processing alone
- Add $30-$110/mo subscription = $240-$320/mo total platform cost = **3-4% of gross revenue**
- With marketplace commissions on 5 new clients/mo at $40 each: add $40-$60/mo

## Undercut Opportunities

| Weakness | Our Opportunity |
|---|---|
| $20-30/staff surcharges (Booksy, Vagaro) | Flat pricing regardless of team size |
| 20-30% marketplace commissions | Lower or zero commission on new clients |
| 2.5-2.75% processing fees | Pass through at cost (~2.2%) or absorb into subscription |
| $2.35 client booking fees (StyleSeat) | Never charge the client |
| Features locked behind $100+/mo tiers | All features included at one price |
| Annual contracts (Boulevard) | Month-to-month only |
| No free tier for solo barbers | Free tier with real functionality (like theCut) |
